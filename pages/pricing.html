Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long should my workout sessions be?

It depends on your goal! A good session lasts 30-60 minutes, including warm-up and cool-down.

Q: What should I eat before a workout?

Carbs + protein! A banana with peanut butter, oatmeal, or a protein smoothie works great.

Q: What is the fastest way to lose belly fat?

No spot reduction! Combine a healthy diet, cardio, strength training, and consistency for results.

Q: Should I stretch before or after my workout?

Dynamic stretching before (to warm up) and static stretching after (to relax muscles).

Q: Can I build muscle without weights?

Yes! Bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and pull-ups can build strength.

Q: How much water should I drink daily?

2-3 liters per day is ideal, but drink more if you're sweating a lot.
